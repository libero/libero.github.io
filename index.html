Libero

Libero is an open-source platform of services and applications to help publishers do more with everything they publish, stewarded by @elifesciences. One instance of Libero drives the eLife journal today. Another instance is being developed by the community, with decision making, coding and deployment all done in the open.

Libero is being built in a modular fashion, from the ground up. The platform is designed to adjust to each journal’s individual needs, to help deliver scholarly content to the highest standards in design and usability, while also providing machine-readable versions and lowering publishing costs. The latest on the development of Libero is available on GitHub.

For more information about eLife, visit https://elifesciences.org/about.
