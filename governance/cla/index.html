Libero Contributor License Agreements

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Libero.

There are two Contributor License Agreements (CLAs) available and you need to choose one depending on the capacity in which you have made your contribution.

Individuals

If you have made your contribution as an individual and not on behalf of a company then please review the Individual Contributor License Agreement and sign digitally at https://libero.pub/sign-cla/.

Entities

If you are an employee, and have made the contribution on behalf of your employer, please have your employer review the Entity Contributor License Agreement then contact the team on libero@elifesciences.org to request a digitally signable copy.

Others

For any other situation or if you have any questions please contact us on libero@elifesciences.org or in the Libero Community Slack.
